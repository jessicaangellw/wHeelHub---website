GR Corolla 2024

Introducing the 2024 Toyota GR Corolla: Performance Perfected

The 2024 Toyota GR Corolla is the latest addition to Toyota’s Gazoo Racing lineup, bringing high-performance engineering and exhilarating driving dynamics to the iconic Corolla nameplate. Built for enthusiasts who crave speed, precision, and an unmatched driving experience, the GR Corolla combines track-proven technology with everyday practicality.

Aggresive and Aerodynamic Design

The 2024 GR Corolla features an aggressive and aerodynamic design that sets it apart from the crowd. Its wide stance, flared fenders, and bold front grille convey its performance pedigree, while functional air vents and a rear spoiler enhance its aerodynamics and stability at high speeds. Available in exclusive GR colors, the GR Corolla's design is both striking and purposeful, ensuring it looks as fast as it drives.

Driver-Centric Interior

Inside, the GR Corolla’s cabin is designed with the driver in mind. Sport seats with enhanced bolstering keep you secure during spirited driving, while premium materials and GR badging add a touch of exclusivity. The cockpit features a digital instrument cluster and an 8-inch touchscreen infotainment system with smartphone integration, providing access to navigation, entertainment, and performance data at your fingertips.

Track-Ready Performance

Under the hood, the 2024 GR Corolla is powered by a turbocharged 1.6-liter three-cylinder engine, delivering an impressive 300 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. This potent powerplant is mated to a six-speed manual transmission, offering precise gear changes and a direct connection to the driving experience. The GR Corolla’s all-wheel-drive system, developed by Gazoo Racing, provides exceptional traction and stability, allowing you to tackle corners with confidence and precision.

Advanced Suspension and Braking

The GR Corolla features a sport-tuned suspension and advanced braking system designed to handle the rigors of both the track and the road. MacPherson strut front suspension and double-wishbone rear suspension ensure responsive handling and a smooth ride, while high-performance brakes with ventilated discs provide reliable stopping power, even under extreme conditions.

Cutting-Edge Technology and Safety

Equipped with the latest in automotive technology, the 2024 GR Corolla offers a suite of advanced driver-assistance features, including adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and automatic emergency braking. The infotainment system supports Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and Amazon Alexa, ensuring you stay connected and entertained on the go. Additionally, the GR Corolla includes Toyota’s Safety Sense™ 3.0, providing peace of mind with features like pre-collision system with pedestrian detection and dynamic radar cruise control.

Experience the Thrill of Driving

The 2024 Toyota GR Corolla is not just a car; it's an invitation to experience the thrill of driving. With its blend of high-performance engineering, cutting-edge technology, and everyday practicality, the GR Corolla is the ultimate expression of Toyota’s racing heritage and commitment to driving excellence. Discover the excitement of the 2024 GR Corolla and elevate your driving experience to new heights.
